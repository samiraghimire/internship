About Meridian

Care built around the person, not just the procedure

Since 1994, Meridian Health has grown from a single community clinic into a full-service hospital network — without losing the bedside manner that started it. Our teams combine modern diagnostics with the kind of attentiveness that makes a hospital feel less like one.

  • Accredited quality care

    Independently reviewed and certified across every major department.

  • 24/7 emergency support

    A fully staffed emergency floor, every hour of every day.

  • 180+ expert physicians

    Specialists trained at leading institutions, based right here.

  • Modern diagnostic labs

    In-house imaging and pathology for same-day answers.
